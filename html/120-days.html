Before you Return

  • Review your vehicle's condition with our excess Wear-and-Use Card
  • Schedule a complimentary pre-inspection
  • Make an appointment with your dealership to turn in your current vehicle
  • Remove any personal items from the vehicle
  • Return the owner's manual, extra keys and other accessories that came with the vehicle.
